Brain Stroke Awareness

A brain stroke occurs when the blood supply to part of your brain is interrupted or reduced, preventing brain tissue from getting oxygen and nutrients. Early treatment is crucial to minimize brain damage and complications.

Symptoms

  • Sudden weakness or numbness on one side of the body
  • Difficulty in speaking or understanding speech
  • Sudden vision problems in one or both eyes
  • Loss of balance or coordination
  • Severe headache with no known cause

Emergency Handling

If you notice stroke symptoms, act FAST ‚Äî Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to hospital. Rush to the nearest stroke care center immediately.

Golden Time (Window Period)

Treatment is most effective if the patient reaches the hospital within 4.5 hours of symptom onset. Every minute counts ‚Äî timely treatment can save brain cells and improve recovery.

Treatment at Ashwini Neuro Center

Our hospital provides 24/7 stroke emergency services, advanced neuroimaging, clot removal (thrombolysis), and expert neurologists available round the clock.
